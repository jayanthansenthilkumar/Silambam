About Silambam

25+ Years of Excellence

Ancient Tamil Martial Art

Silambam is one of the oldest martial arts originating from Tamil Nadu, with a history dating back over 5,000 years. This ancient combat system focuses on using a bamboo staff (called "silam") as the primary weapon.

Our Training Philosophy

Our academy combines traditional Silambam techniques with modern training methodologies to create a comprehensive martial arts experience. We emphasize physical fitness, mental discipline, and spiritual growth.

Physical Fitness

Develop strength, flexibility, agility, and endurance through rigorous training

Mental Discipline

Cultivate focus, patience, and strategic thinking skills

Cultural Heritage

Connect with ancient Tamil traditions and philosophy

Silambam Branches

Explore the diverse branches of Silambam training available at our academy

Pudhu Silambam

Modern Style

A contemporary approach to Silambam that balances traditional roots with competitive applications. Features fluid movements and practical self-defense techniques.

Panjei Silambam

Traditional Style

The most traditional form of Silambam focusing on authentic techniques passed down through generations. Emphasizes discipline and adherence to ancient principles.

Kuravanji Silambam

Performance Art

Combines martial prowess with artistic expression, focusing on choreographed routines and rhythmic movements. Perfect for those interested in both combat and performance.

Kombukkaripudukutthu

Combat Focused

Emphasizes practical combat applications and effective striking techniques. Focuses on developing defensive strategies and real-world self-defense capabilities.

Varimai Silambam

Strength-Based Style

Focuses on developing physical strength and powerful striking capabilities. Builds endurance and physical conditioning through intensive staff training exercises.

Kuthuvarisai

Empty-Hand Techniques

Incorporates empty-hand fighting techniques alongside staff training. Provides a comprehensive martial arts education combining weapons and unarmed combat.
